1. General Rules

  • Tournament Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) par conduct hoga
  • Match custom room me hoga
  • Admin ka decision final hoga
  • Galat details dene par direct disqualification

2. Team Rules

  • Allowed modes: Solo / Duo / Squad
  • Squad me maximum 4 players
  • Extra/substitute player allowed nahi
  • BGMI ID & player name change allowed nahi

3. Slot & Entry Rules

  • Har slot me limited players honge
  • Slot full hone ke baad entry close
  • Entry fee non-refundable
  • Payment ke baad hi slot confirm hoga

4. Match Rules

  • Room ID & Password match se 10 minutes pehle milega
  • Late join karne par re-match nahi milega
  • Emulator / iPad not allowed
  • Hacks / cheats use karne par lifetime ban

5. Point System

Action Points

6. Disqualification Rules

  • Hacking / Mod APK
  • Teaming with enemies
  • Abusive or toxic behavior
  • Fake screenshots / fake claims

7. Room Rules

  • Spawn island par fight allowed nahi
  • Glitches ka misuse strictly banned
  • Spectator mode allowed nahi
  • Match ke beech exit = disqualification

8. Result & Leaderboard

  • Result kills + placement points par based hoga
  • Leaderboard admin ke decision ke baad final hogi
  • Tie hone par higher kills ko preference milegi

9. Prize Distribution

  • Prize sirf verified winners ko milega
  • Payment UPI / Bank Transfer se hoga
  • Fake claim par prize cancel kar diya jayega
